Conversation in Auto-Rickshaw

Common Conversations in Auto-Rickshaw:

  • Idi Pune ki vellutunda? - Will it go to Pune?
  • Station ki velladaniki entha paduthundi? - How much to go to the station?
  • Meeru market ki veltunnara? - Are you going to the market?
  • Nannu MG Road ki theesukellandi. - Take me to MG Road.
  • Ikada nunchi entha dooram undi? - How far is it from here?
  • Mee daggara 500 ki chillar unda? - Do you have change for 500?
  • Nenu mundu kurchocha? - Can I sit in the front?
  • Airport ki velladaniki entha avutundi? - How much to go to the airport?
  • Signal daggara aapandi. - Stop at the signal.
  • Nenu ikada digutanu. - I’ll get off here.
  • Meeru metro station ki theesukellutara? - Can you take me to the metro station?
  • Meeru ikkada vethirinchutara? - Will you wait for me here?
  • Ee dari lo veltunnara? - Are you going this way?
  • Akkada cheradaniki entha time paduthundi? - How long will it take to reach?
  • Konchem tondaraga vellagalara? - Can you go a bit faster?
  • Daggarlo unna hospital ekkada undi? - Where is the nearest hospital?
  • Nenu bus stand ki vellali. - I want to go to the bus stand.
  • Next corner daggara aapandi. - Can you stop at the next corner?
  • Airport ki velladaniki entha charge chestaru? - How much will you charge to go to the airport?
  • Nenu card tho payment chestanu. - I’ll pay by card.
  • ATM ki theesukellutara? - Can you take me to the nearest ATM?
  • Ee dari correcta? - Is this the right way to go?
  • Main road lo vellandi. - Please go through the main road.
  • Daggarlo unna mall ekkada undi? - Where is the nearest mall?
  • Round trip ki entha paduthundi? - How much for a round trip?
  • Rathri prayanam suraksha unda? - Is it safe to travel at night?
  • Nenu rush lo unnanu, tondaraga vellandi. - I’m in a hurry, can you go faster?
  • Nannu gate degara kinda theesukellandi. - Can you drop me at the gate?
  • Okkarike entha charge chestaru? - How much for one person?
  • Mee daggara mobile charger unda? - Do you have a mobile charger?
  • Daggarlo unna restaurant ki entha dooram undi? - How far is the nearest restaurant?
  • Airport ki tondaraga cheralanu. - I need to reach the airport quickly.
  • Ikada aapandi. - Can you stop here?
  • Long trip ki available unnara? - Are you available for a long trip?
  • Konchem cheaper chesthara? - Can you make it cheaper?
  • Nenu na pet tho velagalana? - Can I bring my pet along?
  • Next stop lo digali. - I need to get off at the next stop.
  • Daya chesi ikkada naku wait chesthara? - Please wait for me here.
  • Meeru ela time ki start chestaru? - What time do you start work?
  • Meeru station ki veltunnara? - Do you go to the station?
  • Veri route lo veltara? - Can you take a different route?
  • Back seat lo enni mandi vachu? - How many people can sit in the back?
  • Driver tho kurchocha? - Can I sit with the driver?
  • Nearest hospital ki theesukellutara? - Can you take me to the nearest hospital?
  • Idi shortest route aa? - Is this the shortest route?
  • Meeru market ki veltunnara? - Are you going to the market?
  • Konchem kiraya thaggistara? - Can you reduce the fare a little?
  • Mee daggara chillar unda? - Do you have change?
  • Meeru shortest route tho vellutunnara? - Are you taking the shorter route?
  • Meeru ekkada nunchi vacharu? - Where are you from?
